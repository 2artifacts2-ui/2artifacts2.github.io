Privacy Policy

At our news portal, one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded and how we use it.

Log Files

We follow a standard procedure of using log files. These files log visitors when they visit websites. The information collected by log files includes internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, date and time stamp, and referring/exit pages.

Google AdSense

Some of the ads on our site may be served by Google. Google's use of the DART cookie enables it to serve ads to our site's visitors based upon their visit to our website.
