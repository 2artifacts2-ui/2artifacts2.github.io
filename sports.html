Global Champions League Final: Defending Champions Defeated

| By Editorial Staff

The Global Champions League final concluded with a victory for the challenging side over the reigning title holders following a penalty shootout. Official data confirms record-breaking viewership numbers across digital broadcasting platforms for the event.

The winning team secured the title after the match ended in a draw during regular and extra time. Post-match reports highlight the defensive formation utilized throughout the game as a key factor in the outcome.

Formula 1 Confirms Transition to Sustainable Fuels for 2027

| By Editorial Staff

The FIA has officially confirmed that starting in 2027, all Formula 1 vehicles will transition exclusively to 100% advanced synthetic sustainable fuels. This mandate is part of the organization's objective to achieve Net Zero Carbon by 2030, covering track operations and the wider supply chain.

The new power units will feature increased electrical power, reaching 350kW, utilizing synthetic fuels designed to be carbon-neutral. Teams are currently in the development phase for these regulations, with on-track testing scheduled to commence in late 2026.

Source: FIA - Formula 1 Technical Regulations Update
